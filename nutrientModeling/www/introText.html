Introduction to the Nutrient Modeling Website

This website presents detailed, country-specific information on affordability, food and nutrient availability by food group and adequacy and dietary diversity for scenarios to 2050.

It is based on research by a group of collaborators (see Acknowledgements section) to be published in two papers—one substantive and one technical that includes the code and data. The tentative titles are

  • Potential income growth and climate change effects on global nutrition security to mid-century
  • Modeling nutrition security scenarios: Description of the technical process

The website, and the results, are currently in development, and these tabs are likely to vary with each visit. Please treat this as work-in-progress and do not share. If you find something that seems wrong or is questionable, please see the "For Further Information" tab.

The website has a tabbed interface; one tab for each topic with sub-tabs to provide detail.

The current tabs and subtabs are

  • Food Availability (by food group)
  • Nutrient Availability (by food group)

    • NB—Nutrient Balance Score
    • MRV—Maximum Recommended Intake
